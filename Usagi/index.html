Usagi's Wonderland

Basic information about Usagi

Source:

Japanese manga artist NAGANO's 《ちいかわ(Chiikawa)》

Birthday:

January 22 (Aquarius)

Origin of the name:

The Japanese word "うさぎ" means rabbit, and it is directly transliterated as "Usagi".

Gender:

The official documentation does not specify this, and there is no fixed setting.

Appearance and Weapon

1. Appearance

  • Bright yellow
  • Short round ears
  • Fluffy white tail
  • Inverted V-shaped eyebrows
  • Protruding lower lip that resembles a pout

2. Weapon

  • A long staff in yellow and white
  • with cannons/fireworks launched from both ends
  • Hand-drawn face on the staff

Personality and Language

1. Personality

  1. Full of energy
  2. Loves pranks
  3. Very curious
  4. Extremely calm and reliable in critical moments

2. Language

It doesn't speak, only makes strange noises:

  • "Ura"
  • "Yaha"
  • "Eeya"

Most of the time it relies on Hachime(another character) to relay its words.

Abilities and Interests

1. Occupation/Qualification:

  • Holds a Level 3 weed control certification
  • Outstanding combat talent
  • Quick reflexes, strong mobility
  • Proficient weapon handling

2. Interest

  • Enjoy browsing secondhand shop props
  • Exploring new places
  • Tasting all kinds of delicious food
